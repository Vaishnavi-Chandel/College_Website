R&D COMPUTER ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT

B. Tech Degree

The department has been offering B.Tech. Degree Course in Computer Engineering since 1987 with an intake of 33 students. A sound theoretical and practical work prepares the students in wider field of Computer Engineering to take up challenging jobs in the area of :-

  • Systems Software Development
  • Application Software Development
  • Computer Networking.

The department has also offering B.Tech. Degree Courses in Information Technology since 2006 with present intake of 84 student.
There is 100% placement of all students through campus and our students are placed in reputed IT companies like Microsoft, IBM, TCS, HCL, Hughes, Perrot Systems, DRDO, CMC, VSNL, Wipro, Google, CDOT, Satyam, Oracle, HP, Samsung, Infosys etc.

M. Tech Degree

M. Tech. Degree Course in Computer Engineering (part-time) has been introduced from 1996 for sponsored candidates of the NIT, Kurukshetra.
M. Tech. Degree Course in Computer Engineering (Regular) has been introduced from 2009.

Degree of Doctor of Philosophy

Facilities for research are offered in the department on various topics leading to Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Engineering.

Research Facilities

Faculty is specialized in the department for advanced studies and research exit in the following fields:-

  • Operating Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Distributed Computing
  • Software Engineering
  • Object Oriented Technology
  • Database and Data Mining
